Laam

Laam Coin waa token casri ah oo ku dhisan blockchain-ka Stellar, ujeedadiisuna tahay in uu fududeeyo ganacsiga casriga ah — gaar ahaan iibinta iyo kiraynta guryaha, baabuurta, iyo hantida kale. Laam wuxuu diiradda saarayaa degdeg, kalsooni, iyo isticmaal fudayd.

Waa project furan oo ay heli karaan dhammaan dadka daneeya Web3 iyo decentralized finance (DeFi). Laam Coin waxaa lagu heli karaa adigoo sameeya trustline, waxaana loo qaabeeyey si uu u noqdo mid aad ugu fudud dadka isticmaala wallets sida Lobstr, Solar, iyo Freighter.

Hadafka Laam waa in uu noqdo xalka cusub ee kaabaya nidaamka lacag bixinta ee suuqyada madaniga ah — oo lagu aamini karo, qiimo jaban leh, isla markaana si xor ah loogu ganacsan karo.
